About

Code meets craft meets camera.

I'm a Computer Science MSc student at TU Wien specialising in extended reality and interactive systems. My work sits at the intersection of real-time graphics, embedded systems, and creative technology — I'm as comfortable writing full-stack web projects as I am wiring an Arduino to a vintage VFD display or craeting VR experiences in Unreal Engine.

Outside of code, I shoot street photography across Vienna and beyond — documenting the textures, light, and quiet moments that cities offer. That visual instinct feeds directly back into how I design immersive experiences.
